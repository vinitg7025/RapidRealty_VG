Welcome to Rustomjee Prive

Rustomjee Prive in BKC Annexe, Kala Nagar in Bandra offers lavish 3 BHK apartments. Live exceptionally at the homes in Rustomjee Prive offers exclusive apartments with deck options. The project has a single high-rise tower with three wings, and it has amenities on the 4th-floor podium, including a swimming pool, a kids' play area, and a dedicated senior citizen area. These modern homes have high-quality internal finishes, vitrified tile flooring, a granite kitchen platform, and a stainless-steel sink, making the apartment a space that makes you feel at home. To engage your family further, you can indulge in the world of amenities provided at the rooftop including a basketball court, a yoga pod, a sundown lounge, an amphitheater, and more designed to energize you.
